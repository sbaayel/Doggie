You see a puppy, a person who is blind sees the world! And, at Doggies for the Blind, this journey begins with volunteers. Our mission can only be accomplished with the help of our valued puppy raising volunteers who usher our puppies down the path to becoming guide dogs. We provide a community of support while you are being paid in unconditional love. This is volunteer service that is both warm, and fuzzy!
